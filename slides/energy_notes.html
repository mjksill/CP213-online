Balance equation: Energy

\begin{align*} {\rm accumulation} &= {\rm inflow} - {\rm outflow} + {\rm generation} \end{align*}
  • accumulation: \(\Delta (mU)\)
  • flow terms:
    • convective heat transfer: \(hS(T-T_\infty)\) Δ t
    • radiative heat transfer: \(\sigma S T^{4}\)
    • convection with mass: \(\dot{m} H \Delta t\)
    • work: \(F \Delta x\) or \(- p \Delta V\)
  • generation:
    • chemical reaction: \(\Delta H_{\rm rxn} \Delta\alpha\)
